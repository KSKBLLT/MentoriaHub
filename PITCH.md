# Mentoria Hub — Pitch (Eligibility + Readiness Engine)

**Hook:** _"Stop guessing what you qualify for. Mentoria Hub tells you — and gets you ready."_
**Format:** 7 slides · video ≤ 4:00 · human cold-open · team intro LAST.

---

## THE FIRST 10 SECONDS ⭐ — "The Named Student" (recommended)

Open on a person with the exact pain, then resolve it on screen in 4 seconds. No team intro, no logo, no "our project is a platform that…".

**Frame by frame:**
- **0:00–0:03** — One student, named and located: face/avatar of **Aruzhan, grade 10, a town in Turkistan region**, staring at a messy pile of browser tabs + Telegram channels.
- **0:03–0:05** — Text/VO: *"Dozens of scholarships and olympiads are open to her right now. She knows about two — and isn't sure she even qualifies."*
- **0:05–0:07** — Hard cut to Mentoria Hub: she answers a few questions, and the screen fills with green badges — **"You qualify for 7."**
- **0:07–0:10** — VO: *"In ten seconds, Mentoria Hub answered what she'd been guessing about for a year."*

**Exact words:**
> 🇬🇧 *"This is Aruzhan — grade 10, a town in the Turkistan region. Dozens of scholarships and olympiads are open to her right now. She knows about two, and she isn't sure she even qualifies. Watch what Mentoria Hub tells her in ten seconds."*

> 🇷🇺 *"Это Аружан — 10 класс, посёлок в Туркестанской области. Прямо сейчас ей доступны десятки стипендий и олимпиад. Она знает о двух — и не уверена, что вообще проходит по условиям. Смотрите, что Mentoria Hub скажет ей за десять секунд."*

**Why it lands:** a named, located KZ teenager makes Problem-Understanding + Impact concrete instantly; the green "You qualify for 7" reveal is the product's wow *and* the emotional payoff in one cut — relief from anxiety, not "a list."

### Alternative A — "The Operator's Confession" (highest-leverage for THIS panel, judges are Mentoria operators)
> 🇬🇧 *"You run Mentoria on Telegram. Every week your mentors answer the same question — 'is this opportunity for me?' Here's the version of Mentoria that answers it for a thousand students at once."* → cut to the admin's live scaling counter.
Use for the **live demo** opener; warm tone, not lecturing.

### Alternative B — "Two Students, One Catalog"
Split-screen: the same catalog renders **visibly differently** for grade-9 Social-Impact vs grade-11 Finance. *"Same platform. Two students. The platform already knows what each one qualifies for."* Proves the engine is real (kills the "is it hardcoded?" suspicion).

**Openers that kill momentum (never):** team intro; "in today's world, education…"; reading the rubric; logo splash; screen-recording yourself typing a localhost URL.

## 4-minute video script (timestamped)

- **0:00–0:18 — COLD OPEN (Named Student).** Aruzhan + messy tabs (0:00–0:05) → cut to Hub onboarding → **"You qualify for 7"** green badges (0:05–0:10) → _"In ten seconds, Mentoria Hub answered what she'd been guessing about for a year."_
- **0:18–0:48 — PROBLEM.** _"Mentoria helps students like Aruzhan — today, over Telegram and live calls. But it can't scale, and the hardest question students ask isn't 'what's out there' — it's 'is this for me?' Grade? Citizenship? Language level? They guess. And they miss things they'd have won."_
- **0:48–1:08 — SOLUTION.** _"Mentoria Hub reads each opportunity's real requirements and tells every student exactly what they qualify for — and why. - **3:38–4:00 — VISION + ASK.** _"From eligibility to acceptance — for every student, in every region. Next: deadline reminders, verified portfolios universities can trust, a sponsor view. We're [Team Name]. We made 'is this for me?' disappear. Thank you, Mentoria."_ (Last frame: logo + live URL + email.)
